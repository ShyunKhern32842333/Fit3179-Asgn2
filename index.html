Malaysia Literacy State

This visualization explores education and literacy rates in Malaysia, focusing on key trends and factors over the past two decades. The data examines literacy rates, school enrollment figures, gender disparities in education, and the relationship between government education spending and literacy improvements. By analyzing these factors, the visualization sheds light on Malaysia's progress in education and the impact of investments in literacy on different population groups.

Key Highlights

  • Overall literacy rates across different states
  • School enrollment figures for primary and secondary education
  • Gender disparities in literacy and enrollment
